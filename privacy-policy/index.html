Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026‑04‑13

Developer: One Good Dev

Welcome to .ovpn Hub (“the App”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, how it may be shared, and the choices you have.

1. Who we are and scope

The App is developed and published by “One Good Dev”. This Privacy Policy applies to the .ovpn Hub mobile application and any in‑app features that link to this policy.

2. Age restrictions

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

3. Key assurances about VPN connections

  • The App does not provide or establish a VPN connection.
  • We do not record, store, or log your VPN browsing activity.
  • Your device’s IP address may be used transiently for diagnostics.

4. How the App works (configuration hub)

.ovpn Hub aggregates .ovpn configuration files from public sources and lets you download, share, and transfer those files to OpenVPN‑compatible applications. Example source: VPN Gate.

5. Information we collect

The App is designed without account registration and without collecting VPN traffic data.

5.1 Information you provide voluntarily

Support communications (email address, request content).

5.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device information (model, OS, locale, app version).
  • Transient IP address for diagnostics.
  • Network diagnostics (ping latency).
  • In‑app interaction data (non‑personal).

5.3 Local storage

On‑device cache via Hive CE (server lists, favorites, ping results, downloaded files, UI preferences).

6. Third‑party services and SDKs

  • VPN Gate – fetch public VPN configs.
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) – advertising identifiers, device info, consent via UMP.
  • In‑App Review – native store prompts.
  • Ipify – public IP lookup.
  • OpenVPN‑compatible apps – external VPN connections.

7. Advertising and consent (AdMob)

Consent requested where required (EEA/UK). Respect device‑level ad preferences. Serve non‑personalized ads if declined. Rewarded ads optional for certain features.

8. How we use information

  • Provide core features (.ovpn file handling).
  • Validate VPN servers and measure quality.
  • Diagnose problems and improve reliability.
  • Display advertising (with consent).

9. Data retention

IP addresses not retained beyond diagnostics. Local cache stored until cleared or app uninstalled.

10. International data routing

Traffic may be routed through servers in other countries when using third‑party VPN apps.

11. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used, but no method is 100% secure.

12. Your rights and choices

  • Access, correction, deletion, objection, portability, withdrawal of consent.
  • Reset/limit advertising identifier in system settings.
  • Clear local data or uninstall the App.

13. Third‑party links

The App may contain links to third‑party sites or services. Review their policies before use.

14. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

15. Contact us

  • Developer: One Good Dev
  • Email: one.good.developer@gmail.com

Short summary (for Google Play Data safety mapping)

  • No account is required; the App does not provide VPN connections or handle VPN traffic.
  • Transient IP use for diagnostics; network diagnostics include ICMP ping to VPN endpoints.
  • Local caching on device via Hive CE (server lists, favorites, metrics, .ovpn files) — not uploaded to our servers.
  • Monetization via Google Mobile Ads (AdMob); advertising identifiers and device information may be processed by Google, with consent where required.
  • VPN sources and third‑party VPN apps/servers are outside our control and may have their own logging practices; review their privacy notes.
